How the Index works

The Power Index blends three components into a single 0-100 rating. Each week we recompute from fresh data scraped from the league standings page.

RATING = 40% × ADJUSTED WIN % + 35% × AVG RUN DIFF + 25% × ITERATIVE OPP-ADJ RATING

Adjusted Win % combines your record with your opponents' records (RPI-style). Iterative Rating runs 25 passes over every game, solving for each team's true strength by factoring in the strength of who they beat or lost to — so a close loss to a great team counts more than a blowout of a bad one. In weeks 1-3, run differential is weighted heavier since SOS hasn't stabilized yet.
